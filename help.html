About DeepCariesScan

An AI-powered dental caries detection & classification system.

Version 1.0.0 — Final Year Project

DeepCariesScan uses a trained AI model to analyze dental X-rays and flag possible caries (cavities), classifying each finding across a 5-stage severity scale — from early enamel involvement through to pulp exposure. It's built to give dental practitioners a faster, more consistent second read, not to replace their judgment.

DeepCariesScan is a diagnostic aid, not a final diagnosis. All AI findings should be confirmed by a licensed clinician before any treatment decision.

Frequently asked questions

Is DeepCariesScan a replacement for a dentist's diagnosis?
No. It's a diagnostic aid intended to support a licensed clinician's judgment — every finding should be clinically confirmed before any treatment decision is made.
What image formats and X-ray types are supported?
JPEG and PNG files up to 10MB. Panoramic (OPG), bitewing, and periapical X-rays are all supported.
Are my patients' X-ray images stored after analysis?
No. Uploaded images are analyzed and then discarded. Only the AI's findings, annotations, and any clinician notes you add are saved to a patient's record.
What do the severity stages mean?
DeepCariesScan classifies findings across five stages of increasing severity: Enamel, Dentin 1, Dentin 2, Dentin 3, and Pulp. See the Severity Guide section below for a full explanation of each.
Why does a scan get flagged for review?
Scans are flagged when a finding's confidence falls below your configured threshold (see Settings → AI Preferences), so it gets a second look before being finalized.
Who do I contact if something isn't working?
Use the Contact & Support section below, or reach the KCD IT support desk directly.
